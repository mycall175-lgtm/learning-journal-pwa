PROFILE PICTURE INSTRUCTIONS
============================

To add your profile picture:

1. Place your profile image in this folder (static/images/)
2. Name it exactly: "profile.jpg" (or "profile.png" if it's a PNG file)
3. Recommended size: 300x300 pixels or larger (square image works best)
4. Supported formats: JPG, JPEG, PNG

The app will automatically display your picture in the profile section on the home page.

If the image is not found, a placeholder will be shown.
